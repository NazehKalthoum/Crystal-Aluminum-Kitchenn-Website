Who we are

Crystal Aluminum

It is a Syrian company specialized in Aluminum & Glazing industry as Manufacturer & Installer. Established in 2018 by Mohammad Kalthoum and his partner. Eng. Mohammad Kalthoum had acquired his experience for the past 20 years manufacturing and installing all types of aluminum and glass in Syria, Lebanon, KSA, Qatar and Algeria. Our focus has to meet the specifications of architects, engineers, contractors, developers & End-users.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision is to be the leader in Aluminum & glazing manufacturing field in the region by expanding the marketplace delivering projects and services at your expectation. Our Mission is to present the highest quality materials and workmanship to our customers at a competitive price just in time. Our Goals is to achieve the best strategy by gaining the trust of our entire customer.
